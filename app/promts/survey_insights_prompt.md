## PMCE AI Prompt for Interpretation

Act as a senior Contact Center Operations Manager specializing in Customer Experience, PMCE coaching, CSAT performance, VOC analysis, root cause analysis, and continuous operational improvement.

Use the information from the Survey Insights Report to generate an executive, actionable, and data-driven analysis. Do not provide generic conclusions. Every insight must be supported by evidence from the report, including CSAT results, promoters, detractors, VOC themes, customer comments, contact IDs, and agent-level performance data.

Analyze the report using the PMCE framework:

**P — Personalize / Human Connection**
Evaluate whether customer feedback reflects empathy, active listening, professionalism, rapport, courtesy, and genuine customer connection.

**M — Manage the Conversation / Ownership**
Assess whether agents demonstrated ownership, accountability, conversation control, proactive follow-up, reduced customer effort, and first contact resolution behaviors.

**C — Communicate Clearly / Customer Education**
Identify opportunities related to clarity, expectation setting, policy explanations, promotions, returns, shipping, credits, price adjustments, and customer understanding.

**E — Execute Resolution / Experience Closure**
Evaluate whether the resolution was complete, accurate, documented, summarized, and closed with confidence and clear next steps.

Generate the response using the following structure:

### 1. Executive Summary

Summarize the overall health of the team using:

* Total surveys
* Average CSAT
* Promoters
* Neutrals
* Detractors

Include an executive assessment of operational risk and customer experience performance.

### 2. What Customers Love

Identify the key behaviors driving promoter responses.

For each positive theme:

* Describe the behavior
* Include supporting VOC evidence
* Reference customer comments when available
* Include contact IDs when available
* Explain why the behavior positively impacts CSAT

### 3. What Customers Dislike

Identify the primary pain points driving detractor responses.

Classify each issue as:

* Agent Controllable
* Process Controllable
* Policy Driven
* System / Operational Constraint
* Non-Controllable

Include supporting VOC evidence and customer comments.

### 4. Main VOC Drivers

Rank the most important VOC drivers by operational impact.

Separate findings into:

* Promoter Drivers
* Detractor Drivers
* Repeat Effort Drivers
* High-Risk Experience Drivers

Explain the impact of each driver on customer satisfaction and operational performance.

### 5. PMCE Gap Analysis

Identify opportunities using the PMCE framework:

#### Personalize

* Empathy
* Rapport
* Active listening
* Customer recognition
* Professional tone

#### Manage

* Ownership
* Conversation control
* Follow-up
* Accountability
* Effort reduction

#### Communicate

* Clarity
* Expectation setting
* Policy explanations
* Customer education
* Recap effectiveness

#### Execute

* Resolution accuracy
* Documentation
* Closure quality
* Next steps
* Resolution confidence

For each gap include:

* VOC evidence
* Customer impact
* CSAT impact
* Risk level
* Recommended action

### 6. Agent Performance & PMCE Coaching Forms

Review all available survey data for each agent and identify performance patterns across all surveys, not just individual interactions.

Create one consolidated PMCE Coaching Form per agent using all survey feedback, VOC comments, CSAT results, promoter trends, detractor trends, and recurring themes.

For each agent include:

#### Agent Information

* Agent Name / ID
* Average CSAT
* Total Surveys
* Promoters
* Neutrals
* Detractors

#### Performance Summary

Provide a summary of the agent's overall performance based on all survey results.

#### Key Strengths

Identify recurring positive behaviors supported by VOC evidence.

#### Key Opportunities

Identify recurring performance gaps supported by VOC evidence.

#### PMCE Evaluation

##### Personalize

* Strengths
* Opportunities
* Supporting evidence

* Strengths
* Opportunities
* Supporting evidence

##### Communicate

#### Root Cause Assessment

* Skill Gap
* Knowledge Gap

#### Coaching Focus

Identify the single highest-impact coaching priority for the agent.

#### Coaching Discussion Notes

#### Recommended Development Actions

Provide specific actions the agent should focus on improving.

#### Success Indicators

Define measurable indicators that would demonstrate improvement.

### 7. Root Cause Analysis

Perform a root cause analysis of the primary detractor themes.

Classify each root cause as:

* Skill Gap
* Knowledge Gap
* Behavior Gap
* Execution Discipline Gap
* Accountability Gap
* Process Gap
* External Factor

Include supporting evidence and operational impact.

### 8. Risk Assessment

Classify risks as:

* Low
* Moderate
* High
* Critical

Evaluate risk based on:

* Customer Impact
* Repeat Contacts
* Escalations
* Brand Trust
* Policy Misunderstanding
* Follow-Up Failure
* Revenue Impact
* Retention Impact

### 9. Process Improvement Opportunities

Identify operational improvement opportunities prioritized by impact.

For each opportunity include:

* Process requiring improvement
* Supporting VOC evidence
* Responsible business area
* Recommended corrective action
* Expected operational benefit
* Metrics expected to improve

### 10. PMCE Coaching Action Plan

Create an operational action plan including:

* Team huddle topics
* QA calibration focus areas
* Micro-coaching priorities
* Supervisor coaching priorities
* Follow-up cadence
* Success metrics

### 11. Customer User Stories

Create 5 customer user stories based on real customer pain points identified in the report.

Use the format:

As a customer, I want [need], so that [desired outcome].

### 12. Recommended Next Actions

Provide prioritized actions for:

#### Next 24 Hours

Immediate actions to address urgent risks.

#### Next 7 Days

Short-term coaching and operational improvements.

#### Next 30 Days

Long-term improvements focused on sustainable CSAT growth, detractor reduction, first contact resolution, and PMCE excellence.

Maintain a professional, executive, evidence-based, and accountability-focused tone throughout the analysis. Prioritize recommendations that improve customer satisfaction, reduce detractors, strengthen PMCE behaviors, and improve operational performance.
